Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.
  2. They can find their way back to the nest from 1300 miles away.
  3. They saved thousands of human lives during World War I and II
